Welcome to Dr. Qian Yuan's Research

Dr. Qian Yuan is a leading researcher in geophysics, geochemistry, igneous petrology, and geochronology. Our research focuses on understanding Earth's deep processes, planetary formation, and the evolution of our planet through time.

Research Focus

Our interdisciplinary approach combines geophysical modeling, geochemical analysis, and petrological studies to investigate fundamental questions about Earth's structure and evolution.

Learn More

Current Projects

We are currently investigating the giant impact origin of Large Low Shear Velocity Provinces (LLSVPs), subduction initiation processes, and the geochemical signatures preserved in various mineral systems.
